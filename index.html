Tunnl

NYC Subway Tracker — Real-Time MTA Map & Train Times

About

Tunnl shows you exactly when your train is coming. Open the app, tap a station, see real-time arrivals. No clutter, no ads, no account required.

  • Real-time arrivals for all 496 NYC subway stations
  • Live train positions moving across an interactive map
  • Filter by line to see only your commute
  • Just 7MB — smaller than a single photo
  • No login, no ads, no data collection
  • Works on iPhone, Apple Silicon Mac, and Vision Pro

Privacy Policy

Tunnl does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Specifically:

  • No account or login required
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs
  • No advertising frameworks
  • No data shared with third parties
  • Location (if enabled) is processed entirely on-device to show your position on the map and find nearby stations — it is never transmitted to any server

The only network requests Tunnl makes are to the MTA's public GTFS real-time feeds to retrieve current train positions and arrival times.
